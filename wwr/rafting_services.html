Services

Trips vary from mild and great for families, to trips exclusively for physically fit and experienced rafters. No matter what type of river adventures you are seeking, White Water can make it happen for you.

Boat rental

Our new sets of rafting boats are available for you to make your experience enjoyable and memorable. Our boat is guaranteed to make it through the rough waters. Rafting equipment has continuously evolved and developed significantly from old rubber WW II era military surplus rafts. Our modern whitewater rafts are typically made with advanced nylon or Kevlar infused plastics like PVC or urethane.

Tour Guide

For those exploring water rafting for the first time or tourists looking for an exciting outdoor moment, our tour guide services beat every expectation. We have professional tour guides with years of experience in water rafting who will make your rafting experience memorable. Our tour guides are seasoned professionals with local knowledge of the rivers. We ensure that your safe itineraries and that you are informed in an entertaining manner about the various rivers.

Rafting competition

Every month, we organize and hold a rafting competition for all our members. It's the most exciting event you do not want to miss. People from all walks of life participate in this event, looking forward to it every month. The event holds every last Saturday of the month, with many side attractions. Participants at this monthly event always look forward to it. There are many reasons to be part of this exciting event. From the cash gifts awarded to the winner, and second and third runners up, to the barbecue during the after-party, everyone gets a feel of what an outdoor activity should look like.
